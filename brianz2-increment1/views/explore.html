Our Mission

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. We believe technology and art are a shared language of human expression.

Who We Serve

  • First-time visitors and curious adults
  • Students and educators
  • Families seeking interactive, educational experiences
  • Artists and technologists seeking inspiration
  • Members and donors supporting cultural institutions

Founding & Milestones

MonoMuse was founded in 2008 as a digital-art collective. Key milestones:

  • 2008 — Founded as a digital-art collective
  • 2011 — First permanent gallery space opened
  • 2014 — Expanded to 40,000 sq ft facility
  • 2017 — Launched K–12 education partnerships
  • 2022 — Opened the Innovation Lab maker space
  • 2025 — Celebrated 1 million total visitors
